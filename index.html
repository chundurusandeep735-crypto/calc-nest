Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum attendance required in college?

Most Indian universities and colleges require a minimum of 75% attendance. Some colleges require 80% or 85%. Check with your institution to confirm.

How is attendance percentage calculated?

Attendance % = (Classes Attended ÷ Total Classes Held) × 100. For example, if you attended 60 out of 80 classes, your attendance is 75%.

How many classes can I bunk if I have 80% attendance?

It depends on your total classes and target %. Use the calculator above — enter your numbers to get an exact "safe to bunk" count.

Can I use this for school attendance too?

Yes! This calculator works for any class-based attendance — school, college, university, coaching classes, etc.
